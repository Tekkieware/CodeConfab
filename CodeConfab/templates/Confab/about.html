ABOUT CODECONFAB

CODECONFAB makes coding much more fun. It is a community of dozens of progarammers with varying experience levels in tons of programming languages where people can work together to be better equipped to solve problems while coding. People can interact via one on one chats, make friends, ask for help from friends, paste code and even share links to resources.

INTELLECTUAL IMPACT

Interact with other programmers, let them know when you need help, get the right resources from experts, offer help to others, share your resources and take your knowledge of programming to another level.

SOCIAL IMPACT

Our one vs one chat avenue allows you to have private chat with your authourized friends. That means you can once in a while have a chitchat with an authorized friend.

CODE PASTING

Code Pasting with excellent rendering, an attractive syntax formatting with emphasis on your code's language. People who use your code's language as per their profile definition will be able to see your post and can comment and make corrections. You can poke a friend when you think they can help and also prompt a friend you think it'll be of help to.
